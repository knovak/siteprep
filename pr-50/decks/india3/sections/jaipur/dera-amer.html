Attraction

Dera Amer (Ethical Elephant Experience)

Jaipur

Dera Amer offers a curated elephant interaction and dinner experience, emphasizing ethical care, feeding, and time with rescued elephants.

History & context

The camp was created to provide a more responsible alternative to traditional elephant rides, focusing on care and education.

Visitor details

  • Hours: Experiences are by reservation, with daytime visits and evening dinners available.
  • Address: Dera Amer, near Amer, Jaipur, Rajasthan 302028.
  • Google Map: Dera Amer, near Amer, Jaipur, Rajasthan 302028
  • Distance from Jaipur city center: Roughly 18 km from central Jaipur.

What travelers like

  • Hands-on feeding and care activities.
  • Evening dinner setup in a rural setting.
  • Focus on elephant welfare compared with ride-based tourism.

What travelers dislike or note

  • Premium pricing and limited availability.
  • Must pre-book and confirm ethical practices.
  • Location is outside the city center.
