Attraction

Jawai Leopard Safari

Udaipur

The Jawai region offers leopard safaris in rugged granite hills, with a blend of wildlife viewing and rural village landscapes.

History & context

The Jawai area became known for leopard conservation and community coexistence around the Jawai Bandh reservoir, with tourism growing in recent decades.

Visitor details

  • Hours: Safaris usually run early morning and late afternoon (roughly 6:00–9:00 AM and 3:00–6:00 PM).
  • Address: Jawai Bandh region near Bera, Pali district, Rajasthan 306126.
  • Google Map: Jawai Bandh region near Bera, Pali district, Rajasthan 306126
  • Distance from Udaipur city center: Approximately 150 km from Udaipur (about 3 hours by road).

What travelers like

  • High chance of spotting leopards compared to many parks.
  • Beautiful granite landscapes and village life.
  • Less crowded than major tiger reserves.

What travelers dislike or note

  • Wildlife sightings are never guaranteed.
  • Safaris can be pricey with limited operators.
  • Dusty terrain and bumpy tracks.
