Old City Walking Tour (Pink City)

Jaipur

An Old City walking tour explores Jaipur's Pink City markets, havelis, and historic gates, best enjoyed in cooler hours.

History & context

Jaipur's planned 18th-century grid of bazaars and chowks was designed by Maharaja Sawai Jai Singh II to host crafts and trade routes.

Visitor details

  • Hours: Most tours run early morning or late afternoon; evenings are lively but busier.
  • Address: Common starting points include Hawa Mahal, Tripolia Gate, or Chandpole Bazaar.

What travelers like

  • Colorful bazaars, textiles, and jewelry shops.
  • Architectural details on havelis and gateways.
  • Great street photography and food stops.

What travelers dislike or note

  • Traffic noise and crowds in busy bazaars.
  • Heat can be intense mid-day.
  • Persistent touts around tourist hotspots.
