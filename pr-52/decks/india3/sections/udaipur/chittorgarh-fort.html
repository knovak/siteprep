Attraction

Chittorgarh Fort (UNESCO)

Udaipur

Chittorgarh Fort is one of India's largest forts and a UNESCO World Heritage site, famed for Rajput legends and sprawling ruins.

History & context

The fort served as the historic seat of the Mewar kingdom and is associated with legendary sieges, including those of Rani Padmini and Maharana Pratap's lineage.

Visitor details

  • Hours: Typically 9:00 AM–5:00 PM.
  • Address: Chittorgarh Fort, Chittorgarh, Rajasthan 312001.
  • Google Map: Chittorgarh Fort, Chittorgarh, Rajasthan 312001
  • Distance from Udaipur city center: Around 115 km northeast of Udaipur (about 2.5–3 hours by road).

What travelers like

  • Massive complex with palaces, towers, and temples.
  • Rich Rajput history and heroic legends.
  • Great for architecture and photography.

What travelers dislike or note

  • Large area requires a vehicle or long walks.
  • Limited signage in some sections.
  • Can be hot and exposed in midday sun.
