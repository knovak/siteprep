Ranthambore National Park

Ranthambore (Sawai Madhopur)

Ranthambore National Park is one of India's premier tiger reserves, mixing dry deciduous forest with lakes and the dramatic Ranthambore Fort ruins.

History & context

Once the hunting ground of Jaipur's royal family, the area became a protected tiger reserve and national park, helping revive tiger populations.

What travelers like

  • High-profile tiger sightings and diverse terrain.
  • Historic fort and lake scenery.
  • Good birdlife beyond big cats.

What travelers dislike or note

  • Strict entry limits and advance booking requirements.
  • Heat and dust in warmer months.
  • Sightings are never guaranteed.
