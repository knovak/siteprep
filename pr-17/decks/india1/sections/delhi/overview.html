Overview

Delhi

India's capital region pairs Mughal-era monuments with modern hubs like Connaught Place, Aerocity, and Gurugram. Winter brings some of the most walkable weather alongside occasional morning haze.

Population: ~20 million (metro)
Typical January weather: 7–21°C, cool with foggy mornings
Typical February weather: 10–24°C, clearer skies and pleasant afternoons

Fast facts

  • Highlights: Red Fort, Jama Masjid, India Gate, Lodhi Garden, Qutub Minar, and the National Museum.
  • Transit: Indira Gandhi International Airport (DEL) with Airport Metro Express; an extensive metro network connects major neighborhoods.
  • Food: Street food in Chandni Chowk, kebabs in Nizamuddin, and a wide range of modern dining in Aerocity.
  • Seasonal note: Carry a light jacket for evenings; mornings can be misty in January.
