Attraction

Pushkar Lake

Pushkar

Pushkar Lake is a sacred lake surrounded by dozens of ghats, central to the town's pilgrimage atmosphere.

History & context

The lake is tied to Hindu mythology and has long been a pilgrimage destination, with ghats added over centuries for ritual bathing.

Visitor details

  • Hours: Open all day; sunrise and evening aarti are the most atmospheric times.
  • Address: Pushkar Lake, Pushkar, Rajasthan 305022.
  • Google Map: Pushkar Lake, Pushkar, Rajasthan 305022
  • Distance from Pushkar town center: At the heart of Pushkar (0–0.5 km from the main bazaar).

What travelers like

  • Spiritual ambiance and sunrise reflections.
  • Easy to combine with temple visits and café stops.
  • Photogenic ghats and daily rituals.

What travelers dislike or note

  • Crowds during festivals or holiday periods.
  • Hawkers and touts can be persistent.
  • Respectful dress and etiquette expected around rituals.
