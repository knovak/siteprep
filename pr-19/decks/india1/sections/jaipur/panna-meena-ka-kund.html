Panna Meena ka Kund (Stepwell)

Jaipur

Panna Meena ka Kund is a photogenic stepwell near Amer, known for its symmetrical stair patterns.

History & context

The stepwell dates to the 16th century and once served as a communal water source for the Amer area.
