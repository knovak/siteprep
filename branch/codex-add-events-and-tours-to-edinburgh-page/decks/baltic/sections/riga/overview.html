Overview

Riga

Riga combines walkable Old Town streets with straightforward airport and city-center transport links. Visit Wednesday May 27 to Thursday May 28, sunrise 4:45am, sunset 9:56pm, waxing gibbous.

City stay highlight

  • Wellton Riga Hotel & SPA — Central Old Town hotel with spa facilities, convenient for rail, market, and riverfront walks. Address: Vaļņu iela 49, Centra rajons, Rīga, LV-1050, Latvia. Hotel website | Google Maps

Quick orientation

  • Airport transfer: Taxi, Bolt, and airport bus services connect Riga International Airport and the center.
  • Walkability: Most major Old Town sights are within a short walking radius.

Trails

  • Jugla Lake Forest Loop: 4.6 stars, 5.3 mi, 190 ft gain, Difficulty: Easy. Forest and lakeside route in the city’s northeast green belt with mostly level paths. AllTrails.
  • Mežaparks Woodland Circuit: 4.5 stars, 4.7 mi, 108 ft gain, Difficulty: Easy. Classic Riga urban-forest walk through pine stands and park tracks. AllTrails.
  • Lielupe Riverside Path: 4.4 stars, 6.0 mi, 72 ft gain, Difficulty: Easy. Broad riverside trail near Jūrmala with easy footing and frequent rest points. AllTrails.
  • Baltezers Nature Loop: 4.4 stars, 5.6 mi, 141 ft gain, Difficulty: Moderate. Mixed gravel and woodland paths near the Baltezers lakes, good moderate-distance option. AllTrails.
