Overview

Tallinn

Tallinn's medieval Old Town is compact and easy to navigate, with quick links to port, rail, and airport transfers.

City stay highlight

  • Hotel Regent Tallinn — Small luxury stay in the Old Town core, close to city walls and major squares. Address: Pikk tn 69, 10133 Tallinn, Estonia. Hotel website | Google Maps

Quick orientation

  • Airport transfer: Trams, taxis, and app-based rides connect Lennart Meri Tallinn Airport and the city center.
  • Walkability: Old Town and nearby cultural districts are easy to cover on foot.

Attractions

  • Telliskivi Creative City: Major Tallinn creative quarter with design shops, galleries, food halls, and regular cultural programming in renovated industrial buildings. Location: Telliskivi tn 60a, 10412 Tallinn, Estonia. Official site | Visit Estonia guide | Google Maps.
  • Fotografiska Tallinn: Contemporary photography museum with rotating international exhibitions, talks, and a rooftop-focused dining/bar experience in the Telliskivi complex. Location: Telliskivi tn 60a-8, 10412 Tallinn, Estonia. Official site | Google Maps.
  • Kumu Art Museum: Main building of the Art Museum of Estonia and a major Kadriorg cultural stop, with Estonian art from academic and modernist periods through Soviet Pop and contemporary art. Street address: A. Weizenbergi 34 / Valge 1, 10127 Tallinn, Estonia. Hours: Tu-W, F-Su 10:00-18:00, Th 10:00-20:00, M closed; ticket sales end 30 minutes before closing. Shows to note: Triumph of Galatea: Art in the Age of Artificial Intelligence runs 13 February-9 August 2026 on the 5th floor Contemporary Art Gallery; The Future is in One Hour: Estonian Art in the 1990s is a permanent exhibition on the 4th floor B-wing about post-Soviet transition-era Estonian art. Museum site | Google Maps.

Trails

  • Harku Forest Loop: 4.6 stars, 5.1 mi, 203 ft gain, Difficulty: Easy. Woodland loop on soft trails near Tallinn with gentle grades and year-round access. AllTrails.
  • Paljassaare Peninsula Trail: 4.6 stars, 4.2 mi, 82 ft gain, Difficulty: Easy. Coastal nature walk with birdwatching and sea views, mostly flat and wind-exposed. AllTrails.
  • Nõmme-Mustamäe Nature Trail: 4.5 stars, 5.4 mi, 246 ft gain, Difficulty: Moderate. Pine-forest route over small sandy rises with clear waymarking from city transit links. AllTrails.
  • Pirita River and Forest Trail: 4.4 stars, 4.9 mi, 154 ft gain, Difficulty: Easy. Riverside and forest mix with broad paths, practical for a short urban-nature outing. AllTrails.
