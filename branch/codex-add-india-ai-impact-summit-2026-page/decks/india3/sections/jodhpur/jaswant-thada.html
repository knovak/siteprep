Jaswant Thada

Jodhpur

Jaswant Thada is a serene marble cenotaph complex with landscaped gardens and lake views, often paired with a fort visit.

History & context

Built in 1899 in memory of Maharaja Jaswant Singh II, the memorial is known for its intricately carved marble and royal portraits.

What travelers like

  • Quiet setting and elegant marble craftsmanship.
  • Great photo stop with fort views.
  • Easy add-on to Mehrangarh.

What travelers dislike or note

  • Limited interpretive signage.
  • Short visit length for those wanting deeper history.
  • Midday sun can be intense in the open courtyards.
