Sydney

Sydney has two strong add-ons for this trip window: a major citywide show and an intimate jazz series in one of the city's landmark venues.

Major festival highlight

Sydney Royal Easter Show — One of Australia's largest annual ticketed public events, with agricultural showcases, carnival rides, family attractions, food stalls, and entertainment.
Location: Sydney Showground, 1 Showground Rd, Sydney Olympic Park NSW 2127, Australia
Hours: Opening hours vary by day/program; check the official daily planner.
Dates for the current season: 2-13 April 2026
Links: Official event site | Sydney Showground | Google Maps

Live music add-on

  • Jazz at The Lounge — Smaller-scale jazz concert series with scheduled evening performances in April.
    Location: The Lounge, The Concourse, 409 Victoria Ave, Chatswood NSW 2067, Australia
    Hours: Event-led (example listing: 7:30-9:30 pm sessions)
    Links: 2026 Jazz at The Lounge listing | Venue event page | Google Maps

Planning tips

  • For high-demand dates (school holidays, weekend nights), book tickets as soon as schedules open.
  • If visiting both in one trip segment, use rail to Sydney Olympic Park for the Show and rail/Metro to Chatswood for The Concourse.
