Lake Pichola Boat Ride

Udaipur

Boat rides on Lake Pichola are the classic way to experience Udaipur's island palaces and fort-lined skyline, with options for short circuits or sunset cruises.

History & context

Regular boating started as the city expanded around the lake; the circuit typically loops past Jag Mandir and the Lake Palace, with stops or non-stop sightseeing depending on ticket type.

What travelers like

  • Great, close-up views of Jag Mandir and the Lake Palace.
  • Cooling breezes and sunset light on the Aravalli hills.
  • Easy add-on to an old-city walking loop.

What travelers dislike or note

  • Ticket lines can be long around sunset.
  • Standard boats can feel crowded.
  • Weather or high winds occasionally pause departures.
