Travel essentials

Payment and connectivity notes for India.

Payment cards

UPI-linked wallets and tap-to-pay cards now cover most day-to-day transactions in Indiaâ€™s cities and retail hubs.

  • UPI ONE WORLD prepaid wallets can be activated with passport + visa KYC at partner counters (airports, hotels, designated exchangers), then funded in INR via international card or cash for QR payments nationwide.
  • UPI is now a near-universal payment rail across urban India, from retail stores to restaurants and rides.
  • Visa and Mastercard contactless cards are widely accepted, but expect FX fees and occasional dynamic currency conversion markups.

Travel eSIMs

Most international travel eSIMs that work in India ride on the big local networks (Reliance Jio, Airtel, and Vodafone Idea/Vi), with Jio and Airtel usually offering the best 4G/5G reach.

  • Airalo and Nomad: flexible data plans, easy QR activation, and solid value.
  • Saily: often praised for balanced pricing and coverage across networks.
  • Holafly: best if you want unlimited data and no top-up worries.
