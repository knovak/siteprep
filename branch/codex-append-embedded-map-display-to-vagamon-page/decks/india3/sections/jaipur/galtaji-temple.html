Attraction

Galtaji (Monkey) Temple

Jaipur

Galtaji is a pilgrimage complex set in a rocky valley, famous for its sacred water tanks and resident monkey troops.

History & context

The site has long been a Hindu pilgrimage destination with temples built around natural springs, drawing devotees for ritual bathing.

What travelers like

  • Unique architecture nestled between cliffs.
  • Sacred water tanks and ritual activity.
  • Great for early morning visits and photos.

What travelers dislike or note

  • Monkeys can be aggressive with food or bags.
  • Some areas show wear and litter.
  • Steep steps in parts of the complex.
