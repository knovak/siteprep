Alleppey houseboat

Alleppey (Alappuzha) is Kerala's best-known backwater base, with slow-moving canals, lagoon edges, and palm-lined rice fields that make houseboat stays the classic way to experience the region.

Lakes and Lagoons highlight

Address: Kumarakom, Kottayam, Kerala 686563. Google Maps

Hotel link: lakesandlagoons.com

Description: A tranquil lakeside retreat on the edges of Vembanad Lake, Lakes and Lagoons is designed for quiet backwater immersion with villa-style rooms and lagoon views. The resort reflects the modern wave of Kumarakom hospitality that grew alongside backwater tourism, combining contemporary comforts with Kerala-inspired design motifs.

Houseboat experience notes

Tripadvisor travelers highlight the Lakes & Lagoons Tour Company for private houseboat cruising on Kerala's backwaters, with flexible itineraries that can combine lagoon stretches, narrow canals, and village scenery.

  • Houseboat atmosphere: Reviews frequently mention peaceful, slow-paced cruising that emphasizes scenery, birdlife, and the calm rhythm of the waterways.
  • Onboard hosting: Guests note attentive crew service and freshly cooked meals that lean into Kerala flavors, making the boat stay feel more like a floating homestay.
  • Logistics: Travelers appreciate coordinated pickups/drop-offs and clear communication about routes, timing, and meal preferences ahead of departure.
