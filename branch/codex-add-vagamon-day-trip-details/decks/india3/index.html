Sariska stay highlight

Suggested heritage-style lodge for the Sariska stop.

Hotel: Sterling Rewilded Sariska.

Address: Sariska Tiger Reserve area, Alwar district, Rajasthan 301406.

Description: A nature-focused resort positioned for quick access to safari gates, with low-rise villas and a quiet, wildlife-forward setting.

History: Part of Sterling’s rewilding collection, the property is designed as a modern retreat inspired by the reserve’s conservation landscape.
