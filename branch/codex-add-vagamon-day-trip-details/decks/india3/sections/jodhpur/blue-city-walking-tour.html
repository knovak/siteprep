Blue City Walking Tour

Jodhpur

A Blue City walking tour explores the maze of indigo-painted houses beneath Mehrangarh Fort, with stops at wells, temples, and local homes.

History & context

The blue-painted lanes historically marked Brahmin quarters and later became a signature identity of Jodhpur's old city.

Visitor details
