Overview

Kangaloon

Kangaloon is a quiet Southern Highlands village. It is not a dense wine destination by itself, but there is a strong cluster of nearby cellar doors around Bowral, Mittagong, and Sutton Forest.

Wineries

  • Centennial Vineyards (~10-15 minutes from Kangaloon): One of the best-known Southern Highlands producers with cool-climate wines (including Pinot Noir and Chardonnay), plus an on-site restaurant and regular events.
    Address: 252 Centennial Road, Bowral NSW 2576
    Hours: Daily 10:00-17:00 (cellar door)
    Website: centennial.net.au/cellar-door
    Map: Google Maps
  • Cherry Tree Hill Wines (~15 minutes): A boutique, family-run cellar door with a scenic rural setting and relaxed tastings.
    Address: 12324 Hume Highway, Sutton Forest NSW 2577
    Hours: Daily 10:00-17:00
    Website: cherrytreehill.com.au
    Map: Google Maps
  • Artemis Wines (~15-20 minutes): Known for organic-minded vineyard practices and a casual, dog-friendly atmosphere with tasting-and-picnic style visits.
    Address: 46 Sir Charles Moses Lane, Mittagong NSW 2575
    Hours: Daily 11:00-16:00 (most public holidays)
    Website: artemiswines.com.au/cellar-door
    Map: Google Maps
  • Tertini Wines (~15 minutes): A smaller, quality-focused producer that suits quieter tastings.
    Address: Kells Creek Road (off Wombeyan Caves Road), Mittagong NSW 2575
    Hours: Thu-Mon 10:00-17:00
    Website: tertiniwines.com.au
    Map: Google Maps

Summary

  • Yes, but limited locally: Kangaloon itself is rural with very few wineries directly in the village.
  • Good nearby cluster: Bowral, Mittagong, and Sutton Forest collectively form the practical Southern Highlands wine area for tastings.
  • What to expect: Cool-climate wines, small-to-mid-size cellar doors, and a quieter experience than larger NSW wine regions.
