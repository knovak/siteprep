Inverness and Regional

Inverness is a practical Highland base for regional day trips, with walkable riverfront areas and easy road links toward Loch Ness and the Great Glen.

Expected weather for Inverness (June 10–13)

For these June dates, a practical planning expectation is mild days and cool evenings with intermittent rain. Met Office long-term averages for Inverness in June are around 16.7°C daytime highs, 9.3°C overnight lows, and roughly 52 mm monthly rainfall (about 9 days with rain in June).

  • Pack plan: light waterproof, layers, and one warmer evening layer.
  • Daylight: very long daylight windows in mid-June support extended day-trip timing.
