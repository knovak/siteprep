Attraction

Mehrangarh Fort

Jodhpur

Mehrangarh Fort towers above the Blue City, with expansive ramparts, courtyards, and one of Rajasthan's best fort museums.

History & context

Founded in 1459 by Rao Jodha, the fort expanded over centuries and now houses galleries of weapons, textiles, and royal palanquins.

What travelers like

  • Outstanding museum collections and audio guides.
  • Panoramic views over the blue houses.
  • Multiple gateways and courtyards to explore.

What travelers dislike or note

  • Steep climbs and many steps.
  • Extra fees for photography or special exhibits.
  • Can be hot on exposed ramparts.
