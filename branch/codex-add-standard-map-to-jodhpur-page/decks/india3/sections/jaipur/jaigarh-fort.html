Attraction

Jaigarh Fort

Jaipur

Jaigarh Fort sits above Amber and is known for its massive Jaivana cannon and strong defensive architecture.

History & context

Built in 1726 by Maharaja Sawai Jai Singh II, Jaigarh protected the Amber Palace complex and housed military infrastructure.

What travelers like

  • Panoramic views over Amber and the lake.
  • Jaivana cannon and armory exhibits.
  • Less crowded than Amber in many seasons.

What travelers dislike or note

  • Requires separate transport from Amber Fort.
  • Interior areas are sparse in some sections.
  • Steep climbs between viewpoints.
