Attraction

Brahma Temple

Pushkar

The Brahma Temple is one of the few dedicated to Lord Brahma in India, making it Pushkar's most significant temple stop.

History & context

The current structure dates largely to the 14th century, with a distinctive red spire and silver turtle icon in the inner sanctum.

Visitor details

  • Hours: Typically 6:00 AM–1:00 PM and 3:00–9:00 PM.
  • Address: Brahma Temple Road, Pushkar, Rajasthan 305022.
  • Google Map: Brahma Temple Road, Pushkar, Rajasthan 305022
  • Distance from Pushkar town center: Roughly 0.5 km from Pushkar Lake ghats.

What travelers like

  • Spiritual significance and unique dedication to Brahma.
  • Compact visit that pairs easily with the lake.
  • Photogenic red spire and temple bells.

What travelers dislike or note

  • Crowds at peak times.
  • Photography restrictions inside.
  • Dress and conduct rules are enforced.
