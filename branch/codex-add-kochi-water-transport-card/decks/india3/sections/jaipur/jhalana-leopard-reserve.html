Attraction

Jhalana Leopard Reserve

Jaipur

Jhalana Leopard Reserve is an urban wildlife area offering jeep safaris with good chances of spotting leopards close to Jaipur.

History & context

Once part of Jaipur's hunting grounds, the reserve became a protected area to conserve leopards and manage eco-tourism near the city.

Visitor details

  • Hours: Safaris typically run 6:00–9:00 AM and 3:00–6:00 PM (seasonal).
  • Address: Jhalana Safari Park, Jhalana Institutional Area, Jaipur, Rajasthan 302004.
  • Google Map: Jhalana Safari Park, Jhalana Institutional Area, Jaipur, Rajasthan 302004
  • Distance from Jaipur city center: About 7 km from central Jaipur.

What travelers like

  • Leopard sightings without a long drive.
  • Good birdlife and dry forest scenery.
  • Smaller crowds than major tiger reserves.

What travelers dislike or note

  • Sightings are not guaranteed.
  • Strict safari timing with limited slots.
  • Bumpy tracks in parts of the reserve.
