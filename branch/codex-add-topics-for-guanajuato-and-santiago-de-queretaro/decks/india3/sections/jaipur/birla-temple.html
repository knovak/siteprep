Attraction

Birla Temple (Laxmi Narayan Temple)

Jaipur

The Birla Temple is a modern white-marble temple dedicated to Lakshmi and Narayan, known for its clean lines and peaceful atmosphere.

History & context

Completed in 1988 by the Birla family, the temple blends modern and traditional design with depictions of Hindu deities and philosophers.

Visitor details

  • Hours: Typically 6:00 AM–12:00 PM and 3:00–9:00 PM.
  • Address: Jawahar Lal Nehru Marg, Tilak Nagar, Jaipur, Rajasthan 302004.
  • Google Map: Jawahar Lal Nehru Marg, Tilak Nagar, Jaipur, Rajasthan 302004
  • Distance from Jaipur city center: About 4 km from the old city center.

What travelers like

  • Serene setting and gleaming marble architecture.
  • Good sunset light on the temple exterior.
  • Comfortable visit for those seeking a quieter stop.

What travelers dislike or note

  • Less historic compared to older temples.
  • Limited shade around the grounds at midday.
  • Crowds increase during evening aarti.
