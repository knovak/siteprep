Attraction

Amber Fort Ascent (Elephant or Jeep)

Jaipur

The ascent to Amber Fort can be done by jeep or, for limited hours, by elephant rides, which are controversial and increasingly regulated.

History & context

Elephant rides once formed a ceremonial approach to the fort; today, ethical concerns and regulations have reduced availability and increased scrutiny.

Visitor details

  • Hours: Typically offered in the morning hours; jeep service runs throughout visiting hours.
  • Address: Amer Fort parking and ramp area, Devisinghpura, Amer, Jaipur, Rajasthan 302028.
  • Google Map: Amer Fort parking and ramp area, Devisinghpura, Amer, Jaipur, Rajasthan 302028
  • Distance from Jaipur city center: About 11 km from central Jaipur.

What travelers like

  • Jeep rides are fast and convenient.
  • Views of the fort ramp and lake on the climb.
  • A memorable arrival for first-time visitors.

What travelers dislike or note

  • Elephant rides raise animal welfare concerns; consider skipping.
  • Queues for rides can be long.
  • Price haggling is common.
