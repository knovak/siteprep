Retirement Fund & RMD Model

Models a tax-deferred account (Traditional IRA / 401(k)) under IRS Uniform Lifetime Table RMDs.
Inputs
Years to model 30
Starting balance $1,000,000
Current age 65
Asset growth rate (annual) 6.0%
Target annual total withdrawals $60,000
RMDs follow the IRS Uniform Lifetime Table. RMD start age depends on birth year: born ≤1950 → 72, 1951–1959 → 73, ≥1960 → 75 (SECURE 2.0). Each year, the prior year-end balance grows at the chosen rate; the RMD is computed from the prior year-end balance and withdrawn. An additional withdrawal of max(0, target − RMD) is then taken so total withdrawals reach the target.
