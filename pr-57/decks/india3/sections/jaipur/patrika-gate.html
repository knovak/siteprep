Attraction

Patrika Gate

Jaipur

Patrika Gate is a vibrant, newly built gateway with richly painted arches, popular for photography and quick visits.

History & context

Built in the 2010s as part of the Jawahar Circle development, the gate showcases motifs inspired by Rajasthan's palaces and textiles.

Visitor details

  • Hours: Open all day; early morning is best for photos and fewer crowds.
  • Address: Jawahar Circle, Malviya Nagar, Jaipur, Rajasthan 302017.
  • Google Map: Jawahar Circle, Malviya Nagar, Jaipur, Rajasthan 302017
  • Distance from Jaipur city center: Around 9 km from central Jaipur.

What travelers like

  • Bright colors and repeating patterns for photos.
  • Quick stop near the airport side of town.
  • Open public space for short visits.

What travelers dislike or note

  • Crowded with photo shoots later in the day.
  • Limited shade in the circular park area.
  • No historical exhibits on site.
