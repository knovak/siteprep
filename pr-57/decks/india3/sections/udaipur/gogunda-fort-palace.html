Attraction

Gogunda Fort Palace (Heritage Experience)

Udaipur

Gogunda Fort Palace is a restored heritage fort-hotel offering a glimpse of Mewar-era architecture, often experienced as a lunch stop or overnight stay.

History & context

The fort dates back to the 16th century and once served as a temporary capital of Mewar during the reign of Maharana Pratap.

Visitor details

  • Hours: Day visits are typically by reservation; overnight stays allow broader access.
  • Address: Gogunda Fort Palace, Gogunda, Rajasthan 313705.
  • Google Map: Gogunda Fort Palace, Gogunda, Rajasthan 313705
  • Distance from Udaipur city center: About 36 km northwest of Udaipur (around 1 hour by road).

What travelers like

  • Heritage ambiance and restored courtyards.
  • Quiet countryside setting away from city crowds.
  • Good stop on a Kumbhalgarh route.

What travelers dislike or note

  • Limited access if you are not a guest.
  • Experiences can be expensive.
  • Fewer activities beyond the heritage stay.
