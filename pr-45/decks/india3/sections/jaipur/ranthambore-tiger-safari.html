Tiger Safari (Shared or Private Jeep)

Ranthambore (Sawai Madhopur)

Ranthambore tiger safaris are run in open jeeps (gypsies) or canters, with shared or exclusive bookings into designated zones.

History & context

Safari operations were formalized as the reserve grew, with regulated zones and a permit system to manage wildlife tourism pressure.

What travelers like

  • Open-top viewing for better photography.
  • Private jeeps allow more flexibility.

What travelers dislike or note

  • Canter vehicles are crowded and less agile.
  • Last-minute permits are hard to secure.
  • Long drives without guaranteed sightings.
