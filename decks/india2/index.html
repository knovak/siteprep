Late January–Early February 2026 festivals

Highlights across Kerala from Jan 31 to Feb 6, 2026.

  • Mathrubhumi International Festival of Letters (Thiruvananthapuram): Literary talks, author sessions, and cultural programming at Kanakakkunnu Palace, Jan 29–Feb 1.
  • 38th Kerala Science Congress (Kochi): Public-facing science exhibits and lectures alongside the academic congress, Jan 30–Feb 2.
  • Kochi-Muziris Biennale (Fort Kochi + venues): Ongoing contemporary art installations, performances, and talks running through Mar 31.
  • Kanichukulangara Devi Temple Festival (Alappuzha): Multi-week temple rituals and cultural processions, with Chikkarakkottikkal Koottakalam on Feb 2.
  • Thaipooyam Mahotsavam (Thrissur + Harippad): Feb 1 temple festivities featuring kavadi processions, ceremonial music, and devotional observances.
  • Ma Literature Festival (Malappuram): Literature panels, poetry, music, and workshops, Jan 30–Feb 2.
