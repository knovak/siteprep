Overview

Kochi

Kochi (Cochin) is a port city in Kerala known for its backwaters, spice trade legacy, and blend of Portuguese, Dutch, and British influences visible around Fort Kochi and Mattancherry.

Population: ~0.7 million (city)
Typical January weather: 23–31°C, warm with sea breeze
Typical February weather: 24–32°C, humid and sunny

Fast facts

  • Highlights: Chinese fishing nets, St. Francis Church, Jew Town and the Paradesi Synagogue, and Kathakali performances.
  • Backwaters: Day cruises to Vembanad Lake and routes toward Alleppey start from nearby jetties.
  • Transit: Cochin International Airport (COK) is ~35 km northeast; metro and ferries connect Ernakulam and Fort Kochi.
  • Food: Seafood, appam with stew, and Kerala-style biryani headline local cuisine.
