Backwaters of Alappuzha

Alleppey (Alappuzha) is Kerala's best-known backwater base, with slow-moving canals, lagoon edges, and palm-lined rice fields that make houseboat stays the classic way to experience the region.

Resort Lakes and Lagoons

A tranquil lakeside retreat on the edges of Vembanad Lake, designed for quiet backwater immersion with villa-style rooms and lagoon views. The resort reflects the modern wave of Kumarakom hospitality that grew alongside backwater tourism, combining contemporary comforts with Kerala-inspired design motifs.

Houseboat experience

Tripadvisor travelers highlight the Lakes & Lagoons Tour Company for private houseboat cruising on Kerala's backwaters, with flexible itineraries that can combine lagoon stretches, narrow canals, and village scenery.

  • Atmosphere Peaceful, slow-paced cruising that emphasizes scenery, birdlife, and the calm rhythm of the waterways
  • Onboard hosting Attentive crew service and freshly cooked meals that lean into Kerala flavors — feels more like a floating homestay than a tour boat
  • Logistics Coordinated pickups/drop-offs and clear communication about routes, timing, and meal preferences ahead of departure
