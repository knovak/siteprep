Practical notes February

  • Peak season February is one of the best months to visit Kerala — pleasant and mostly dry, great for backwaters and hills alike
  • Ghats caution Hill roads toward Munnar and Vagamon are twisty; get local advice from your hotel or driver each morning before departing
  • Booking Book through the RT travel desk or RT-listed operators to ensure a larger share of your money reaches the community directly

RT experiences

Village Life Experience

A half-day package showing real village life: a canoe (vallom) or canal boat ride, traditional activities like fishing and craftwork, paddy field walks, and engaging with locals in their everyday work. Starts late morning for a few hours up to most of a day.

A Day with Farmers

Full-day immersion in agricultural rhythms: coconut-tree climbing, toddy tapping, coir making, net fishing, farm visits, paddy field walks, traditional craft demonstrations, and canal cruising — all led by local hosts. Meeting points typically around Kavanattinkara/Naalupang near the backwaters.

Kerala's RT model is not just a tour — it's a collaboration with villagers and self-help groups that turns your visit into a source of livelihood, pride, and cultural preservation.
