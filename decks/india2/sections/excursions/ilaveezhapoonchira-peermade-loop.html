Ilaveezhapoonchira first

Ilaveezhapoonchira open meadows

Summary: Rolling grasslands on a treeless saddle with long horizons and shifting cloud views.

Address Ilaveezhapoonchira Check Dam, Meenachil, Kottayam, Kerala · Map
  • Soft hike Follow jeep tracks or footpaths 30–90 minutes round-trip on uneven but gentle terrain
  • Seasonality Lushest after monsoon; golden and expansive in drier months — fog and wind can roll in fast
  • Access note Final approach is rough laterite/stone — high-clearance vehicle and a driver familiar with the road are best, especially after rain

Peermade Plateau tea country

Summary: Tea estates, eucalyptus, and spice gardens with cooler air and wide viewpoints.

Address Peermade, Idukki, Kerala 685531 · Map
  • Easy options Stroll estate roads, pause at viewpoints, plan a relaxed lunch stop
  • Optional add-ons Seasonal waterfalls, spice gardens, or heritage bungalows (view-only unless arranged)

Suggested flow

  • Start early for morning light at Ilaveezhapoonchira
  • Continue to Peermade for a tea-estate stroll and lunch
  • Return via plateau roads before dusk for clearer visibility

Practical tips

  • VehicleSUV/high-clearance recommended; avoid very low sedans
  • WeatherBring layers; wind and fog can roll in quickly
  • FootwearTrail shoes or sturdy sneakers (grassy, sometimes slick)
  • SuppliesCarry water and snacks — facilities are sparse near Ilaveezhapoonchira
  • SafetyStay on visible tracks and avoid wandering far in low visibility
