Kumarakom

Peermade Plateau loop

A gentle countryside loop that pairs high meadows with tea country. It delivers big Western Ghats views, soft walks, and a relaxed pace without a strenuous trek.

Total time: ~6–7 hours with stops
Driving: ~2–2.5 hours each way
Best order: Ilaveezhapoonchira → Peermade

Ilaveezhapoonchira (open meadows)

Summary: Rolling grasslands on a treeless saddle with long horizons and shifting cloud views.

Address: Ilaveezhapoonchira Check Dam, Ilaveezha Punchira, Meenachil, Kottayam, Kerala, India. Google Maps

  • Soft hike: Follow jeep tracks or footpaths for 30–90 minutes round-trip on uneven but gentle terrain.
  • Seasonality: Lushest after monsoon; golden and expansive in drier months. Fog and wind can roll in fast.
  • Access note: The final approach is rough laterite/stone — a high-clearance vehicle and a driver familiar with the road are best, especially after rain.

Peermade Plateau (tea country)

Summary: Tea estates, eucalyptus, and spice gardens with cooler air and wide viewpoints.

Address: Peermade, Peerumade, Idukki, Kerala 685531, India. Google Maps

  • Easy options: Stroll estate roads, pause at viewpoints, and plan a relaxed lunch stop.
  • Optional add-ons: Small seasonal waterfalls, spice gardens, or heritage bungalows (view-only unless arranged).

Suggested flow (from Kottayam/Kumarakom)

  • Start early for morning light at Ilaveezhapoonchira.
  • Continue to Peermade for a tea-estate stroll and lunch.
  • Return via plateau roads before dusk for clearer visibility.

Practical tips

  • Vehicle: SUV/high-clearance recommended; avoid very low sedans.
  • Weather: Bring layers; wind and fog can roll in quickly.
  • Footwear: Trail shoes or sturdy sneakers (grassy, sometimes slick).
  • Supplies: Carry water and snacks — facilities are sparse near Ilaveezhapoonchira.
  • Safety: Stay on visible tracks and avoid wandering far in low visibility.
