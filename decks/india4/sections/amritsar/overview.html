Overview

Amritsar

Amritsar is Punjab's spiritual and cultural capital, famed for the shimmering Golden Temple complex and a lively Old City food scene. It pairs well with Delhi as a short flight or train excursion.

Population: ~1.4 million (metro)
Typical January weather: 5–20°C, cool mornings
Typical February weather: 8–23°C, sunny and dry

Fast facts

  • Highlights: Golden Temple (Harmandir Sahib), Akal Takht, Jallianwala Bagh, and the Partition Museum.
  • Evening ceremony: The Wagah-Attari border retreat ceremony is a popular late-afternoon excursion.
  • Food scene: Try Amritsari kulcha, lassi, and the Old City's street-food lanes near Hall Bazaar.
  • Transit: Sri Guru Ram Dass Jee International Airport (ATQ) connects to Delhi and major Indian hubs; the rail station is a short drive from the Golden Temple area.

Planning notes

  • Temple visits: Early mornings are calmer; modest dress and head coverings are required.
  • Weather: February days are comfortable for walking tours; evenings can still be chilly.
  • Day trips: Wagah border is ~30 km away; plan transport in advance for the ceremony crowd.
