    • Gate closures: Whitehall Gate closes at 10:00am. All other entrances close by 11:00am. Late arrivals may be redirected and may be denied access.
    • Entrances and routing:
      • Whitehall Entrance: via Charing Cross Station, Embankment, or Westminster.
      • North Side: via Trafalgar Square from Charing Cross Station or Embankment.
      • South Side: via St James’s Park from Petty France & Birdcage Walk or Westminster.
    • Transport hubs: Nearest railway stations are Charing Cross, Waterloo, and Victoria. Nearest Underground stations are Westminster, Embankment, Charing Cross, St James’s Park, and Victoria.
    • Security checkpoint rule: You must use the correct security checkpoint for your stand (North, East, or South).
    • Viewing options (no-ticket and hybrid):
      1. Stand along The Mall: Main free viewing option for procession atmosphere and pageantry between Buckingham Palace and Horse Guards Parade. Public guidance allows standing from 9:00am, but earlier arrival is recommended for better spots.
      2. St James’s Park edge: Official free area overlooking Horse Guards Parade, but views may be limited or obscured by troops.
      3. RAF fly-past from The Mall: Scheduled for 1:00pm; useful even if the formal parade view is partial.
      4. BBC + in-person atmosphere: Watch the formal ceremony live on BBC, then head toward The Mall/Buckingham Palace area for the procession and fly-past depending on day-of crowd controls.
