Overview

London

London is the United Kingdom’s capital and largest city, built around the Thames and known for major museums, royal sites, theater districts, and dense multi-modal transport.

Population

  • Greater London (administrative area): about 8.9–9.1 million residents.
  • Metropolitan area: roughly 14 million residents.
