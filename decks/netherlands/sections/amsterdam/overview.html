Overview

Amsterdam

Amsterdam combines historic canal belts, major museums, bike-first mobility, and an active events calendar from late spring into summer.

Population

  • Municipality of Amsterdam: about 920,000–940,000 residents.
  • Metropolitan region: roughly 2.4–2.5 million residents.

Expected weather for May 31–June 2

For May 31 to June 2, plan for typical late-spring to early-summer Amsterdam conditions: mild days, cooler evenings, and intermittent rain risk.

  • Typical range: around 11–20°C.
  • Pack plan: light layers, comfortable walking shoes, and a compact waterproof shell.

Events

  • Sun 31 May — Lorenzo Viotti leads Rachmaninoff (14:15–16:35): In the Royal Concertgebouw’s Main Hall, Lorenzo Viotti returns with the Netherlands Philharmonic Orchestra for a program centered on Rachmaninoff’s Symphonic Dances, Op. 45, with additional works including Shostakovich’s Violin Concerto No. 2. Ticket prices vary.
  • June 3–28 — Holland Festival: Amsterdam’s major international performing arts festival with theater, dance, music, and interdisciplinary productions. Program and booking: hollandfestival.nl/en.
