Art of Living Ashram

The Art of Living International Center sits on a 65-acre campus at Kanakapura Road outside Bengaluru. Founded by Sri Sri Ravi Shankar in 1986, the ashram hosts yoga, meditation, and Sudarshan Kriya breathing programs that now reach millions worldwide.

The Meera Vanam Premium Executive Suite offers a quiet, hotel-style retreat with upgraded rooms, curated wellness touches, and easy access to the ashram campus for programs or satsangs; learn more about the stay options on Sri Sri Wellbeing and explore the broader retreat accommodations via Art of Living.

Founded: 1986
Location: ~35 km south of central Bengaluru
Campus features: Vishalakshi Mantap, green campus, residential programs
History: The center began as a small retreat to formalize Art of Living breathing techniques and soon expanded into a global headquarters supporting humanitarian, education, and rural development initiatives in over 150 countries.

Visitor notes

  • Programs: Regular Happiness Program, meditation retreats, Ayurvedic consultations, and volunteer opportunities.
  • Access: Ride-hailing services and taxis are the most direct; allow extra time for traffic on Kanakapura Road.
  • Stay: On-campus accommodation and vegetarian dining are available for registered participants.
  • Atmosphere: Quiet, garden-like grounds with evening satsangs and lake views.

Relevant notes

  • Ashram stay: Private hotel-style wellness property where visitors can meet Gurudev.
  • On-site reminders: Mosquitoes; cool weather; eat at the international cuisine upstairs.
  • Treatment options: 5, 7, or 10 day treatments; eye treatments are good over 7 days.
  • Event planning: Watch for Gurudev events; special seating for seniors and international visitors.
  • Contacts: She knows the most popular pulse reader doctor; call the Marma teacher who knows Bangalore well.
