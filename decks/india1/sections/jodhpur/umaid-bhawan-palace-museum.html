Attraction

Umaid Bhawan Palace Museum

Jodhpur

The Umaid Bhawan Palace Museum showcases a slice of royal life inside one of India's last great palaces, with Art Deco interiors and vintage cars.

History & context

Constructed between 1928 and 1943, the palace blended Indo-Saracenic and Art Deco styles and remains partly a royal residence and luxury hotel.

Visitor details

  • Hours: Typically 9:00 AM–5:00 PM (often closed on Tuesdays).
  • Address: Circuit House Road, Jodhpur, Rajasthan 342006.
  • Distance from Jodhpur city center: About 4–5 km from the old city and Clock Tower market.

What travelers like

  • Elegant Art Deco details and royal memorabilia.
  • Beautiful grounds and architecture.
  • Good stop for those interested in 20th-century palace history.

What travelers dislike or note

  • Only a small portion of the palace is open to visitors.
  • Photography restrictions in some galleries.
  • Ticket price can feel high for the museum size.
