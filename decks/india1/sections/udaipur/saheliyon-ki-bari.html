Attraction

Saheliyon Ki Bari (Garden of the Maidens)

Udaipur

Saheliyon Ki Bari is a landscaped garden with fountains, lotus pools, and marble pavilions created as a leisure space for royal women.

History & context

Designed in the 18th century for the queen and her attendants, the garden reflects Mewar-era aesthetics with imported fountains and carved marble kiosks.

Visitor details

  • Hours: Typically 9:00 AM–7:00 PM.
  • Address: Panchwati, Udaipur, Rajasthan 313001.
  • Google Map: Google Map
  • Distance from Udaipur city center: About 2.5–3 km northeast of the old city and City Palace area.

What travelers like

  • Lotus pools, shaded walkways, and calm atmosphere.
  • Compact layout that's easy to visit in 45–60 minutes.
  • Pleasant in the morning or late afternoon.

What travelers dislike or note

  • Some fountains may be inactive depending on water supply.
  • Can feel small if you've seen larger Mughal gardens.
  • Vendors near the entrance can be persistent.
