Attraction

Bagore-ki-Haveli

Udaipur

Bagore-ki-Haveli is an 18th-century mansion on Gangaur Ghat that now houses a museum and hosts nightly folk dance performances.

History & context

Built for a Mewar noble, the haveli showcases period rooms, costumes, and household artifacts, preserving the architectural character of Udaipur's lakefront havelis.

What travelers like

  • Compact museum with authentic rooms and artifacts.
  • Evening dance show highlights multiple folk styles.
  • Great lakefront location for photos.

What travelers dislike or note

  • Photography fees can be high.
  • Show tickets sell out in peak season.
  • Narrow staircases inside the haveli.
