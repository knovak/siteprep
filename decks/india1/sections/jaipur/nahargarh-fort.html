Nahargarh Fort

Jaipur

Nahargarh Fort overlooks Jaipur from the Aravalli hills and is a popular sunset viewpoint with a small palace complex inside.

History & context

Originally built in 1734 by Maharaja Sawai Jai Singh II, the fort later expanded to include the Madhavendra Bhawan palace suites.

  • Sweeping city views, especially at sunset.

  • Good place for photography and evening light.

What travelers dislike or note

  • Narrow, winding road to reach the fort.
  • Can be crowded at sunset.
  • Limited interpretation on-site.
