Attraction

Hawa Mahal (Palace of Winds)

Jaipur

Hawa Mahal is Jaipur's most photographed façade, a honeycomb of windows that once allowed royal women to observe street life unseen.

History & context

Commissioned in 1799 by Maharaja Sawai Pratap Singh, the palace is a five-story façade of pink sandstone with over 900 latticed windows.

Visitor details

  • Hours: Typically 9:00 AM–4:30 PM.
  • Address: Hawa Mahal Road, Badi Choupad, Jaipur, Rajasthan 302002.
  • Google Map: Google Map
  • Distance from Jaipur city center: Around 2 km from central Jaipur and the MI Road area.

What travelers like

  • Iconic exterior for photos, especially in morning light.
  • Views of the bazaar from upper levels.
  • Quick visit that pairs with a Pink City walk.

What travelers dislike or note

  • Interior spaces are narrow and modest.
  • Crowds around the main photo viewpoint.
  • Limited shade on the surrounding streets.
