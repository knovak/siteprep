Rajasthani Folk Dance Show (Udaipur)

Udaipur

Udaipur's folk dance shows spotlight Rajasthan's traditional music, puppetry, and fire or pot-balancing acts, often staged in heritage havelis or cultural centers.

History & context

These performances draw on centuries-old folk traditions from the Mewar region and neighboring desert communities, adapted for evening cultural programs.

Visitor details

  • Hours: Most shows run in the evening, typically 7:00–9:00 PM; schedules vary by venue.
  • Address: Common venues include Bagore-ki-Haveli (Gangaur Ghat) and Shilpgram (near Fateh Sagar Lake).
  • Google Map: Google Map
  • Distance from Udaipur city center: 1–4 km from the old city center depending on venue.

What travelers like

  • High-energy performances and colorful costumes.
  • Short, easy-to-attend evening program.
  • Good way to sample multiple folk styles in one sitting.

What travelers dislike or note

  • Touristy atmosphere at some venues.
  • Limited seating or visibility if you arrive late.
  • Audio amplification can be loud in smaller halls.
