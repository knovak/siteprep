Wildlife Viewing in Ranthambore

Ranthambore (Sawai Madhopur)

Beyond tigers, Ranthambore's landscape supports leopards, sloth bears, sambar, spotted deer, crocodiles, and a wide variety of birds.

History & context

The park's lakes and forested valleys have long supported prey species, making it a strong habitat for predators and a notable birding location.

Visitor details

What travelers like

  • Mix of mammals, birds, and crocodile sightings.
  • Beautiful terrain with lakes and fort backdrops.
  • Good chances to see deer and langurs even without tiger luck.

What travelers dislike or note

  • Zones are assigned randomly on permits.
  • Hot, dusty conditions in late season.
  • Viewing requires patience and long drives.
