Attraction

Amber (Amer) Fort

Jaipur

Amber Fort is Jaipur's most iconic hilltop fortress, known for its ornate halls, mirrored interiors, and sweeping views over Maota Lake.

History & context

Built in the late 16th century by Raja Man Singh I and expanded by successive rulers, Amber served as the Kachwaha dynasty's capital before Jaipur was founded.

What travelers dislike or note
