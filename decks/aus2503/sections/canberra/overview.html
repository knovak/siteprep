Overview

Canberra

Canberra offers major festivals, national cultural institutions, and easy access to nearby nature reserves, making it a strong hub before or after South Coast travel.

Events

  • National Folk Festival: A multi-day Easter music and dance festival featuring Australian and international folk artists, workshops, and markets. Official site: folkfestival.org.au. Venue: Exhibition Park in Canberra (EPIC), address Flemington Road, Mitchell ACT 2911. Map: Google Maps.

Planning notes

  • EPIC is north of the city centre in Mitchell, so allow extra time for event-day traffic and parking.
  • Autumn evenings can cool quickly in Canberra; bring a warm layer for outdoor performances.
