Canberra

Canberra offers major festivals, national cultural institutions, and easy access to nearby nature reserves, making it a strong hub before or after South Coast travel. This page now also checks the requested April 2-5, 2026 additions against live venue and market information.

  • National Folk Festival: A multi-day Easter music and dance festival featuring Australian and international folk artists, workshops, and markets. Official site: folkfestival.org.au. Venue: Exhibition Park in Canberra (EPIC), address Flemington Road, Mitchell ACT 2911. Map: Google Maps.
  • Capital Region Farmers Market: Verified for Saturday, April 4, 2026, which matches the market's standard Saturday schedule. The official market site lists it as running every Saturday, 7:00 am-11:30 am at EPIC, making it a strong morning option during this stay window.
