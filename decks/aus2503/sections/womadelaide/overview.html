WOMADelaide (2026)

WOMADelaide is Adelaide's signature world music and arts festival, staged in Botanic Park and combining international performances with food, workshops, and cultural showcases.

Location: Botanic Park / Tainmuntilla, Adelaide
2026 timing: Typically held over four days in early March (Thu–Sun), with day and evening sessions
Typical March weather: 15–29°C, warm afternoons, cooler nights, low humidity

What to expect in 2026

  • Program mix: Global music stages, contemporary dance, and visual arts installations.
  • Family areas: KidZone activities, hands-on workshops, and shaded play spaces.
  • Food village: Street-food stalls plus local South Australian producers.
  • Festival logistics: Plan for reusable water bottles, picnic rugs, and a mix of sun and evening layers.

March planning tips

  • Bring a hat and sunscreen for daytime sets; switch to a light jacket after dark.
  • Adelaide evenings can cool quickly, especially in parklands.
  • Check the official 2026 schedule and ticket releases when announced.
